Privacy Policy

Hoop Keys is a mobile puzzle and basketball game. This policy explains what information may be processed by the app, why it is used, and how users can contact us.

Information We May Process

  • Email address: Used for email/password sign-in, account creation, and password reset.
  • User ID: Used to keep each player's progress, scores, coins, and game state separate.
  • Player name: May be shown on profile and leaderboard screens.
  • Gameplay progress: Levels, scores, time, move count, coins, boosters, and similar in-game data may be stored.
  • Leaderboard data: Score and performance information may be used for daily, weekly, monthly, and overall rankings.

Guest sign-in does not require an email address. However, an anonymous or session-based user ID may be created to keep progress separate.

How We Use Information

  • To provide account creation, sign-in, and password reset features.
  • To let players continue from their saved progress.
